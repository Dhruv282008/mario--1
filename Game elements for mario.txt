Game Elements:
	
	1.Characters:
		1.Enemies(NPC).
		2.Mario(PC).
	2.Feedback:
		a.Sounds
		b.Scores
	3.Goal - Reach the end safely without losing any life.
	4.Rules:
a.Control Mario using arrow keys.
b.Collect coins.
c.Limited lives(3).
d.Lose 1 life , if Mario touches the enemy.
e.Reach the other level only after completing the previous level.
	5.Story
	6.Balance - Gain 1 more life , after touching the floating lives above the ground.
	7.Adaptivity - Introduce Mario to a new place/enemy if the mario has a power pack or a fly pack
	8.Chance vs skill:
		a.Chance - Motion of the enemies.
		b.Skill - Controlling the mario.
